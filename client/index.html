About

First, the user can enter a topic or keyword in a search field to generate the poem. For example, the user can enter keywords such as "love", "rain", "autumn", "sadness", "joy", "beginning", etc.
The site then uses an artificial intelligence algorithm or database to generate the poem. This can be an entirely new poem created by the algorithm, or a poem made up of fragments of pre-existing poems that are reorganized into a new form.
Depending on the site, the user may have the option to modify the generated poem, to make adjustments to the rhyme or style, or to add or remove lines.
The Site may also provide options for sharing generated poetry via social media
